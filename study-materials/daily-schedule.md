# Daily & Weekly Study Schedule

## Your Profile
- Available time: 30–60 min/day
- Goal: Work in Germany + C1/C2 certification
- Style: All modes (apps + books + video + speaking)

---

## Principle: 5 Daily Inputs
Every study session should touch at least 3 of these 5 skills:

| Skill | Why It Matters |
|-------|---------------|
| Vocabulary (Wortschatz) | Words are the foundation — no words, no communication |
| Grammar (Grammatik) | Structure — stops fossilized errors at higher levels |
| Listening (Hören) | Trains your ear before you ever speak |
| Speaking (Sprechen) | The output that actually gets you the job |
| Reading/Writing (Lesen/Schreiben) | Needed for exam + professional life |

---

## Daily 30-Minute Plan (Minimum Days)

```
[0:00–0:05]  Anki flashcard review (yesterday's cards + due cards)
[0:05–0:15]  DW Nicos Weg episode (watch, pause, repeat key phrases)
[0:15–0:25]  Duolingo or Babbel (one lesson)
[0:25–0:30]  Write 3 sentences using today's new vocabulary
```

## Daily 60-Minute Plan (Full Days)

```
[0:00–0:10]  Anki review + add 10 new cards
[0:10–0:25]  Video/podcast input (DW, Easy German, Coffee Break German)
[0:25–0:40]  Grammar lesson or textbook exercise
[0:40–0:55]  Writing practice or reading a short article
[0:55–1:00]  Shadow 1 paragraph (mimic native pronunciation)
```

## Anki Setup Guide

### Install Anki
- Desktop: ankisoftware.com (free)
- Mobile: AnkiDroid (Android, free) / AnkiMobile (iOS, paid ~$25)

### Download Starter Decks
Go to ankiweb.net/shared/decks/ and search:
- "German A1 Core" — for absolute basics
- "German Core 2000" — for systematic vocabulary
- "Nicos Weg A1" — matches the DW video course

### Card Format (create your own for best retention)
**Front:** German word + article (for nouns)
**Back:** Hindi meaning + example sentence + pronunciation hint

Example:
- Front: `der Bahnhof`
- Back: `रेलवे स्टेशन | Ich fahre zum Bahnhof. | BAHN-hof`

## Shadowing Technique (for Pronunciation)

This is the single most effective technique for accent reduction.

**How to do it:**
1. Choose a 30-second audio clip (DW Nicos Weg works well)
2. Listen once — just listen, don't speak
3. Listen again — read the transcript simultaneously  
4. Listen a third time — whisper along
5. Play it and speak simultaneously, mimicking rhythm and intonation
6. Record yourself — compare to original

**Do this for 10 minutes every day for 3 months → accent dramatically improves.**

## Motivation Tips (for Hindi/Marathi speakers)

1. **Set a concrete date** — register for the Goethe A1 exam 4 months from now. A deadline creates urgency.
2. **Find German in your world** — German cars (BMW, Mercedes, VW), Bayer medicines, Siemens electronics. German is already around you.
3. **Use the language, don't just study it** — after Month 2, write your grocery list in German, set your phone to German.
4. **Accept imperfection** — Germans appreciate ANY attempt at their language. They won't laugh.
5. **Connect with the Indian-German community** — many Indians work in Germany. Find them on LinkedIn, Reddit (r/germany), Facebook groups.
6. **Deutsche Welle is your friend** — Germany's international broadcaster makes the best free German learning content on the internet. Use it constantly.
